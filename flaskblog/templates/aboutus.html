ABOUT US

C.U.B.E is VIT's first and the only cubing club that was established in the year 2019. Our aim is to provide a platform for students who are into speedcubing and give them the opportunity to get better and give recognition to one of the most underrated talents of rubiks cube solving! With a team of over 50+ members with 4 domains, we work on organising cubing events on various scales!
