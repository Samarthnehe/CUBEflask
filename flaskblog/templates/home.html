ABOUT US

Established in 2019, we are C.U.B.E, VIT's first and the only cubing club. Our aim is to support fellow speecubers by providing them a paltform to showcase their underrated skills in the feild of speedcubing. A club with over 50 members including speedcubers, designers and managers, we work on organising events that benefits our college and its students!
